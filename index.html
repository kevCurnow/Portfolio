About

After graduating from Butler University in May of 2017 with a degree in Marketing and Information Systems, I determined I needed a bit more experience before entering the workforce. I had always enjoyed programming courses, but never considered pursuing it as my career.

I enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy's twelve week intensive .NET track and had a fantastic experience. Not only did I develop new technical skills utilizing languages such as C# and Angular, but I met great people as well. I'm so excited and looking forward to my life's next chapter.

Outside of programming, I have a multitude of interests. I'm a pop culture nerd, avid sports fan, curious globetrotter, and love spending time with friends and family. I could talk for days about movies, college basketball, soccer, bucketlist destinations, or favorite commercials.
